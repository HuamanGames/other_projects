Wide Area Networks

What is a WAN?

Wide Area Networks are created when two or more LANs
are connected.

How are WANs connected together?

WANs can be connected together via the telephone system
or can be connected via fibre optic cables, satellites or
microwave links.

What is the largest WAN in the world?

The largest Wide Area Network in existence is the Internet.
