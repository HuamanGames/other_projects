Local Area Networks

What is a LAN?

Local Area Networks are usually computers that are in close
together so that joined together to form a local network.

Where would LANs be used?

LAN's would be used in places like home,
an organisation, a school, a library etc...

Who owns the LAN?

Someone who understands how the LAN works
owns it. They are responsible for the hardware,
the cables and the network itself.

How do you access the LAN?

To access a LAN you usually have to be connected by wires and have a user name and password to log in.
